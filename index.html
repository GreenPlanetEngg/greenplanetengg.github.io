Who we are

We are a team of passionate people, having industrial experiance of more than 20 years in maintenance and servicing of all kinds of electrical, electronics, knewmatic, PLC and CNC drives.

Our goal is to have zero downtime due to machine failures, we provide all kinds assistance and service to achieve the goal. We are known by prompt service. We specialize in emergency machine breakdowns, troubleshooting, control design, and software integration. To insure our local customers meet their production schedules we provided Rapid Response Service. Our goal is to provide our customers with innovative solutions that maximize uptime, simplify operations, and minimize costs. Please contact us for more info.
